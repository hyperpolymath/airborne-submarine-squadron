Seven threats — three natural clusters

Which cluster should we build first? Pick one.

1

Drifting Hazards

Passive objects in fixed orbital zones — no AI, just physics and placement.

Asteroid Belt Debris Clouds Rogue Comets

Why first

  • Simplest to implement
  • Immediately adds navigational tension
  • Reusable "hazard object" system for mines too

Trade-off

  • Passive — no escalating challenge
2

Energy Hazards

Timed environmental threats that force route planning around the solar system.

Solar Flares Radiation Belts

Why first

  • High dramatic impact
  • Builds on existing solar heat system
  • Changes how players route between planets

Trade-off

  • Needs clear visual communication or it feels unfair
3

Combat Threats

Active enemies and traps — reuses atmospheric combat systems in orbital space.

Enemy Patrol Fleets Space Mines

Why first

  • Most engaging — active threat
  • Orbital combat is a new gameplay dimension
  • Mine system is simple standalone feature

Trade-off

  • Most complex — needs enemy AI in orbital space
