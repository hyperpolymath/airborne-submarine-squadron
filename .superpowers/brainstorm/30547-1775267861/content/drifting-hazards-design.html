Solar System Layout
Threat Zones
Asteroid Belt
  • ~60 asteroids, orbitRadius 615–780
  • Radii 4–18, each in stable circular orbit
  • HP: small=1, medium=2, large=3 hits
  • Large/medium split into 2–3 fragments on death
  • Collision: damage proportional to closing speed
Debris Clouds
  • 3–5 clouds at fixed orbital positions
  • Each = 15–25 slow-drifting particles
  • No HP — damage on contact
  • Fly slow through them: collect hull repair / ammo
  • New clouds spawn from large asteroid kills
Rogue Comets
  • 2 comets, parametric elliptical orbits
  • Perihelion near Sun, aphelion past Pluto
  • Fast near Sun, slow at edges
  • Visible trail (last 30 positions)
  • 1 hit = heavy damage; not breakable
Orbital Projectiles

New space.projectiles[] array. Each projectile starts with ship velocity + fire velocity, then gravity is applied every frame (same loop as ship). Torpedoes fast+short-lived, missiles slower+longer range, depth charges wide arc. All affected by all bodies' gravity. Collide with asteroids (deal damage), debris clouds (clear particles), and enemies (future).
