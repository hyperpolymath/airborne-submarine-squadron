# Gameplay Guide

## Objective
Navigate your submarine through challenging environments, collect power-ups, avoid obstacles, and complete missions.

## Controls
- **Movement**: Arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →)
- **Primary Fire**: Spacebar
- **Secondary Fire**: Shift
- **Toggle Environment**: Tab
- **Pause**: Esc

## Power-ups

### Health Pack
- **Effect**: Restores 25 health points
- **Duration**: Instant
- **Appearance**: Red cross symbol

### Ammo Refill
- **Effect**: Replenishes all ammunition
- **Duration**: Instant
- **Appearance**: Yellow ammo crate

### Shield
- **Effect**: Temporary invincibility
- **Duration**: 300 ticks
- **Appearance**: Blue energy field

### Speed Boost
- **Effect**: Doubles movement speed
- **Duration**: 600 ticks
- **Appearance**: Green lightning bolt

### Rapid Fire
- **Effect**: Halves weapon cooldown
- **Duration**: 900 ticks
- **Appearance**: Orange burst

### Double Damage
- **Effect**: Doubles weapon damage
- **Duration**: 600 ticks
- **Appearance**: Purple explosion

### Environment Control
- **Effect**: Toggle between air/water
- **Duration**: 1200 ticks
- **Appearance**: Rainbow swirl

## Obstacles

### Rock
- **Behavior**: Solid, indestructible
- **Effect**: Stops movement
- **Appearance**: Gray boulder

### Iceberg
- **Behavior**: Destructible (100 health)
- **Effect**: Slows movement
- **Appearance**: White/blue ice

### Coral Reef
- **Behavior**: Passable but slows
- **Effect**: 30% speed reduction
- **Appearance**: Pink coral

### Whirlpool
- **Behavior**: Pulls submarine
- **Effect**: Continuous pull force
- **Appearance**: Swirling water

### Thermal Vent
- **Behavior**: Pushes submarine
- **Effect**: Continuous push force
- **Appearance**: Rising bubbles

### Minefield
- **Behavior**: Explosive
- **Effect**: Instant damage
- **Appearance**: Red mines

### Oil Spill
- **Behavior**: Visibility reducer
- **Effect**: Darkens screen
- **Appearance**: Black slick

## Scoring

- **Enemy Destroyed**: 100 points
- **Power-up Collected**: 50 points
- **Obstacle Avoided**: 20 points
- **Mission Completed**: 1000 points

## Tips

1. **Use environment control** to navigate obstacles
2. **Collect power-ups** before tough sections
3. **Prioritize shields** when health is low
4. **Speed boosts** help avoid obstacles
5. **Rapid fire** is great for boss fights

## Mission Types

### Training
- **Objective**: Learn controls
- **Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Difficulty**: Easy

### Survival
- **Objective**: Survive as long as possible
- **Duration**: Unlimited
- **Difficulty**: Medium

### Time Trial
- **Objective**: Complete in record time
- **Duration**: 120 seconds
- **Difficulty**: Hard

### Boss Battle
- **Objective**: Defeat the boss
- **Duration**: Until victory
- **Difficulty**: Expert
