Power-up System Development Notes
==================================

## Implementation Details

### Type System
- Used enum PowerUpType for type safety
- Each variant has specific behavior
- Duration tracked in ticks (60 ticks = 1 second)

### Spawning Logic
- Random spawning every 60 ticks (15% chance)
- Checks for available slots (a, b, c)
- Different probabilities for each type

### Effects
- HealthPack: +25 health (max 100)
- AmmoRefill: Full ammo restore
- Shield: Blocks damage for 300 ticks
- SpeedBoost: 2x speed for 600 ticks
- RapidFire: 2x fire rate for 900 ticks
- DoubleDamage: 2x damage for 600 ticks
- EnvironmentControl: Toggle env for 1200 ticks

## Balancing Notes

### Spawn Rates
- Current: 15% chance every 60 ticks
- Tested: 10-25% range works well
- Adjust based on difficulty

### Duration
- Shield: 300 ticks (5 seconds)
- SpeedBoost: 600 ticks (10 seconds)
- RapidFire: 900 ticks (15 seconds)
- DoubleDamage: 600 ticks (10 seconds)

### Probabilities
- HealthPack: 20%
- AmmoRefill: 20%
- Shield: 15%
- SpeedBoost: 15%
- RapidFire: 15%
- DoubleDamage: 10%
- EnvironmentControl: 5%
