Obstacle System Development Notes
==================================

## Implementation Details

### Type System
- Used enum ObstacleType for type safety
- Each type has unique behavior
- Environment-specific obstacles

### Spawning Logic
- Random spawning every 120 ticks (20% chance)
- Checks for available slots (a, b, c, d)
- Environment-aware spawning

### Behaviors

#### Air Obstacles
- Rock: Solid, indestructible
- Minefield: Explosive on contact

#### Water Obstacles
- Iceberg: Destructible (100 health)
- CoralReef: Slows movement (30%)
- Whirlpool: Pulls submarine toward center
- ThermalVent: Pushes submarine upward

### Environment Logic
- Air: 50% Rock, 50% Minefield
- Water: 30% Iceberg, 30% CoralReef, 20% Whirlpool, 20% ThermalVent

## Balancing Notes

### Spawn Rates
- Current: 20% chance every 120 ticks
- Tested: 15-30% range works well
- Adjust based on difficulty

### Health Values
- Iceberg: 100 health (destructible)
- Other obstacles: Indestructible

### Effect Strength
- CoralReef: 30% speed reduction
- Whirlpool: 5 units/pull
- ThermalVent: 10 units/push
